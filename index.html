About

The Hunt for the Impossible Bottle

I'm Joseph — a bourbon obsessive based in Chicago who started NeatSipper to document the bottles that most people will never taste. Not as a flex. As a record.

Every release I cover — from Weller Antique 107 to George T. Stagg to Pappy Van Winkle — is captured with ASMR-style audio, deliberate pours, and tasting notes that go deeper than the label.

This isn't a rating site. It's a love letter to wheated mash bills, impossible allocations, and the community of people who genuinely care about what's in the glass.
